During this semester at UEF, I took classes related to Data Science, such as Bayesian Inference or Learning Analytics, but also Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning, without forgetting Web Programming.
This experience really developed my soft skills and organisation.

This first year at EMSE learnt me to work with a different approach, using multidisciplinary resources as well as cooperation.
I participated to a Citizen Project ("Projet Citoyen") within a team of 8 persons, using the agile method. This project was realised in partnership with the "Ligue pour la Protection des Oiseaux" (LPO) and aimed at developing audio walks in the Parc de Montaud in Saint-Etienne. We all enjoyed this adventure and hope for this tool to be developed even more for the next years!
This project made me want to learn more about project methodologies, working in teams and leadership, which was possible thanks to the Toolbox (a category of courses at EMSE, that we can choose according to our preferences and/or professional project) called Project Management. The class was held in English. My other Toolbox was Calculation High Performances ("Calcul Hautes Performances") and taught me the basics of multi-threading and GPU programmation.
In addition to those courses, we developed our general engineering skills by strengthing and expanding our knowledge related to various themes in mathematics, physics, environment and IT.

Those were two years that learnt me how to cope with short deadlines, hardworking and managing time to rest.
My first year was in the Physics - Chemistry - Engineering Science field ("Physique, Chimie, Sciences de l'Ingénieur", also known as PCSI) while my second year was in the PC* (Physics - Chemistry) class of Fermat ("classe étoilée en Physique-Chimie").
